FEATURE SEMANTICS (FOR REASONING ONLY)

Use the following to reason about which features are useful. Do NOT modify feature names based on this section.

SIGNAL TYPES:
- Ratings (elo_rating, rpi_rating, days_rest): baseline team strength and rest. Slow-moving, high-stability.
- Averages (avg_*): rolling mean performance. Higher is usually better, except for turnovers, penalties, points_allowed, and sack_yards_lost (where lower is better).
- Trends (trend_*): direction of change (momentum). Positive = improving, negative = declining.

TIME HORIZON:
- L3: very recent form, high variance, fast to react.
- L5: balance between recency and stability.
- L7: more stable, slower to react.

LOCATION CONTEXT:
- *_home and *_away: location-specific performance; home for comfort/advantage, away for resilience.

OFFENSE / DEFENSE / OVERALL:
- Offensive features describe scoring and efficiency.
- Defensive features describe ability to limit opponents.
- Point differential features summarize overall net performance.

TRADEOFF GUIDANCE:
- Stability vs responsiveness (ratings vs short windows).
- Level vs direction (averages vs trends).
- General strength vs situational effects (overall vs home/away).
